Hi, I’m Anni — an admin support specialist who brings order, clarity, and calm to the parts of your business that get messy when you’re busy. I take the overdue, the half‑finished, and the “I’ll sort it later” tasks off your plate so you can focus on the work that actually matters.

### What I do
I handle the admin that quietly piles up in the background: documents, data, inboxes, systems, workflows, and all the digital clutter that slows everything down. My job is simple — keep your operations running smoothly and consistently.

### The human bit
I’m not the corporate type. I work with real people juggling real workloads, and I understand how quickly admin chaos builds up when you’re running a business. I’m warm, practical, and very human about it — no judgement, no fuss, just steady support and a sense of humour when it’s needed.

### How I work
My approach is calm, structured, and methodical. I break work into micro‑steps to prevent overwhelm, communicate clearly without jargon, and build systems that make sense for *you*, not for “best practice” blogs. Everything adapts to your pace and your workflow so the support feels natural, not forced.

### In short
I help you get organised, stay organised, and finally breathe again.
